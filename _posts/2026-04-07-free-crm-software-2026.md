## What is a CRM?

Before diving into the top picks, let's briefly discuss what a CRM is and why it's essential for small businesses. A CRM is a software solution that helps manage customer interactions, track sales performance, and analyze marketing efforts. It enables businesses to store customer data, automate tasks, and gain valuable insights to improve decision-making.
