## What is a VPN?

Before we begin our comparison, let's briefly explain what a VPN is. A VPN is a service that creates a secure and encrypted connection between your device and a VPN server, masking your IP address and protecting your online activity from prying eyes. This is especially important for people who want to access geo-restricted content or stay safe while using public Wi-Fi networks.
