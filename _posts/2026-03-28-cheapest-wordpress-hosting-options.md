**What Makes a Good WordPress Host?**

Before diving into our top picks, let's discuss what makes a good WordPress host. A reliable web host should offer:

1. **Fast loading speeds**: A fast website is essential for user experience and search engine rankings.
2. **Scalability**: Your hosting plan should be able to handle increased traffic as your site grows.
3. **Security**: Look for hosts with robust security features, such as automatic backups and malware scanning.
4. **Support**: Excellent customer support is crucial in case you encounter any issues.
