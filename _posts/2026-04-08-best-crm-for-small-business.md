**What is a CRM?**

Before we dive into the best CRMs for small business, let's define what a CRM is and how it can benefit your company:

A CRM (Customer Relationship Management) tool is software designed to help businesses manage their interactions with customers, clients, or prospects. By storing all customer data in one place, you can:

* Keep track of leads, contacts, and sales opportunities
* Automate routine tasks, such as follow-ups and email campaigns
* Analyze sales performance and make data-driven decisions
