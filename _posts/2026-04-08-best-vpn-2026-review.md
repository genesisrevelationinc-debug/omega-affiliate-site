## What is a VPN?

Before we dive into the reviews, let's briefly explain what a VPN is. A VPN creates a secure, encrypted connection between your device and a server, masking your IP address and protecting your online activities from prying eyes. With a VPN, you can browse the internet anonymously, access geo-restricted content, and safeguard sensitive data.
