**What is a VPN, and Why Do I Need One for Streaming Netflix?**

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is a service that encrypts your internet traffic, making it difficult for hackers or third-party surveillance to intercept your data. When it comes to streaming Netflix, a VPN helps you bypass geo-restrictions, which are limitations imposed by Netflix on content availability based on your location. This means you can access Netflix's vast library from anywhere in the world, regardless of where you're physically located.
