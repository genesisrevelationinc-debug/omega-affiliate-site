A good starting point for any SEO strategy is identifying relevant keywords. Here are some top-notch keyword research tools:

### 1. Ahrefs

* Includes backlink analysis and content optimization suggestions
* Offers a robust keyword planner with export options

Cons:

### 2. Moz Keyword Explorer

Moz's Keyword Explorer tool is another excellent option for keyword research, providing data on search volume, competition, and SERP analysis.
