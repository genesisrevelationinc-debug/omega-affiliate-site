## Understanding Cryptocurrency Exchanges

Cryptocurrency exchanges act as intermediaries between buyers and sellers of digital currencies. They provide a platform for users to trade various cryptocurrencies against fiat currencies or other altcoins. When selecting an exchange, it's essential to consider factors such as security, fees, user interface, and customer support.

### Security Features to Look For

Reputable exchanges prioritize security above all else. Some key features to look for include:

*   **Two-factor authentication (2FA)**: This adds an extra layer of protection to prevent unauthorized access to your account.
*   **Cold storage**: Exchanges should store the majority of their funds in cold storage, protecting them from hacking attempts.
*   **Regular security audits**: A good exchange will conduct regular security audits to identify and fix vulnerabilities.
