# Nordvpn Vs Expressvpn Comparison: Which VPN Reigns Supreme?

**NordVPN**

**ExpressVPN**

### Security Comparison

Security is a top priority when it comes to choosing a VPN provider. Both NordVPN and ExpressVPN employ strong encryption protocols to protect user data.

**NordVPN**

* **Encryption:** AES-256-GCM with double encryption

	+ WireGuard

**ExpressVPN**

* **Encryption:** AES-256-CBC
