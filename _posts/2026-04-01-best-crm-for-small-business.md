In this article, we'll explore the best CRM for small businesses, highlighting their key features, pricing plans, and user reviews. We'll also provide a comparison table to help you make an informed decision.

## Understanding CRMs: What's in a Name?

Before diving into our top picks, let's quickly define what a CRM is and why your business needs one:

* A Customer Relationship Management system is software that helps businesses manage interactions with customers, partners, and suppliers.
* CRMs typically include contact management, sales pipeline tracking, marketing automation, and customer support features.

## Choosing the Right CRM: Factors to Consider

When selecting a CRM for your small business, consider the following factors:

* **Scalability:** Will the CRM grow with your business or become too complex?
* **Ease of use:** Is the interface user-friendly, even for non-technical staff members?
* **Integration:** Does the CRM integrate seamlessly with other tools and apps you already use?
* **Pricing:** What's the total cost of ownership (TCO) per month/year?
* **Support:** What kind of customer support does the vendor offer?
