# Best CRM For Small Business: Top Picks & Reviews

As a small business owner, managing customer relationships is crucial to success. A Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system helps you keep track of interactions with potential and existing customers. However, with numerous options available, choosing the right CRM can be overwhelming. In this article, we'll guide you through the top CRMs for small businesses, highlighting their features, pricing, and pros/cons.

## What is a CRM?

Before diving into the best CRMs, let's quickly understand what a CRM is. A CRM system is designed to manage all your customer data in one place, making it easier to:

* Track interactions with customers
* Automate sales processes
* Analyze customer behavior and preferences
* Enhance customer experience

## Top Picks for Small Business CRM

### 1. HubSpot CRM - All-in-One Solution

HubSpot is a leading CRM platform that offers an all-in-one solution for small businesses. With its free plan, you can manage up to 1 million contacts, making it ideal for startups and growing companies.

* **Pricing:** Free (limited features), $50/user/month (billed annually) for the Starter Plan
* **Key Features:**
	+ Contact management
	+ Sales pipeline tracking
	+ Marketing automation
	+ Customer service tools

HubSpot is a great option due to its user-friendly interface, robust features, and affordable pricing. Try HubSpot's free plan today [here](https://hubspot.sjv.io/).

### 2. Zoho CRM - Affordable & Feature-Rich

Zoho CRM offers an array of features at an affordable price point, making it a popular choice among small businesses.

* **Pricing:** $12/user/month (billed annually) for the Standard Plan
* **Key Features:**
	+ Contact management
	+ Sales pipeline tracking
	+ Marketing automation
	+ Social media integration

Zoho CRM's free plan allows you to manage up to 10 users, making it an excellent option for small teams.

### 3. Pipedrive - Sales-Focused CRM

Pipedrive is a sales-focused CRM that helps small businesses streamline their sales process and close deals faster.

* **Pricing:** $29/user/month (billed annually) for the Essential Plan
* **Key Features:**
	+ Sales pipeline tracking
	+ Deal management
	+ Customizable workflows
	+ Integration with other tools

## Pros and Cons of Each CRM

### HubSpot:

* **Pros:**
	+ User-friendly interface
	+ Robust features at an affordable price
	+ Excellent customer support
* **Cons:**
	+ Limited customization options in the free plan
	+ Steeper learning curve for advanced features

### Zoho CRM:

* **Pros:**
	+ Affordable pricing
	+ Feature-rich with a wide range of integrations
	+ Customizable workflows
* **Cons:**
	+ Limited customer support in the free plan
	+ Can be overwhelming due to its vast feature set

### Pipedrive:

* **Pros:**
	+ Sales-focused features for faster deal closure
	+ Customizable workflows and integration options
	+ Excellent customer support
* **Cons:**
	+ Pricing can be steep for small businesses with limited budgets
	+ Limited contact management capabilities
