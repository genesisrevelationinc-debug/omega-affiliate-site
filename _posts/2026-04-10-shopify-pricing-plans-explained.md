# Shopify Pricing Plans Explained

As an e-commerce entrepreneur, choosing the right platform for your online store can be a daunting task. With numerous options available, it's essential to understand the pricing plans of popular platforms like Shopify. In this article, we'll delve into Shopify's pricing structure, exploring its three main plans: Basic Shopify, Shopify, and Advanced Shopify.

**What is Shopify?**

Before diving into the pricing plans, let's briefly discuss what Shopify offers. As one of the leading e-commerce platforms, Shopify provides a comprehensive suite of tools for creating, managing, and growing an online store. With Shopify, you can design and customize your store, manage products and inventory, process payments, and fulfill orders – all in one place.

**Shopify Pricing Plans**

Shopify offers three main pricing plans, each catering to different business needs:

### 1. Basic Shopify

The Basic Shopify plan is ideal for new or small businesses with limited product offerings (up to 2 staff accounts). For $29 per month (plus payment processing fees), you'll get access to:

* Online store with up to 2 staff accounts
* 2,000+ products
* Shipping labels and rates
* Basic reports

If your business grows beyond these limitations, consider upgrading to a higher plan.

### 2. Shopify

The Shopify plan is suitable for growing businesses with more extensive product offerings (up to 5 staff accounts). For $79 per month (plus payment processing fees), you'll enjoy:

* Online store with up to 5 staff accounts
* 10,000+ products
* Gift cards and COA reports
* Advanced shipping rates

This plan offers more features than the Basic Shopify plan but still has some limitations.

### 3. Advanced Shopify

The Advanced Shopify plan is designed for large businesses or enterprises requiring advanced features (up to 15 staff accounts). For $299 per month (plus payment processing fees), you'll get:

* Online store with up to 15 staff accounts
* No product limit
* API access and webhooks
* Advanced reporting and analytics

This plan offers the most comprehensive feature set, making it suitable for large businesses or those requiring extensive customization.

**Additional Fees**

In addition to the monthly pricing plans, Shopify charges additional fees for payment processing (2.9% + 30¢ per transaction) and gateway setup ($0-$10). There may be also some extra costs for things like fraud analysis, currency conversion, and so on.

When choosing a plan, consider your business's growth potential, product offerings, and staff requirements.
