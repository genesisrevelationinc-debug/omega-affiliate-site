**What to Look for in Cheap WordPress Hosting**

Before diving into our top picks, it's essential to understand what makes a good cheap WordPress hosting plan. Here are some key factors to consider:

* **Uptime**: Ensure the host offers reliable uptime, with minimal downtime or outages.
* **Speed**: A fast loading speed is crucial for user experience and search engine rankings.
* **Security**: Look for hosts that offer robust security features, such as free SSL certificates, firewalls, and malware scanning.
* **Scalability**: Choose a host that can grow with your website's needs.
