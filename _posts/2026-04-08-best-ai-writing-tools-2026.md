## What are AI Writing Tools?

Before diving into the top picks, let's define what AI writing tools are. These software applications utilize machine learning algorithms to assist writers in generating, editing, and refining content. They can help with tasks such as:

* Researching topics and finding relevant information
* Generating ideas and outlines
* Drafting articles and blog posts
* Editing and proofreading
