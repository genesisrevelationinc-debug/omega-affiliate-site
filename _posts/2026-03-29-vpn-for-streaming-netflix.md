**What is a VPN?**

A VPN creates a secure, encrypted connection between your device and a server. This allows you to mask your IP address, making it appear as though you're accessing the internet from a different location. When it comes to streaming Netflix, a VPN can help you:

* Bypass geo-restrictions: Access content not available in your region
* Improve security: Protect yourself from hackers and cyber threats
* Enjoy uninterrupted viewing: Reduce buffering and loading times

**What Makes a Good VPN for Streaming?**

When choosing a VPN for streaming, consider the following factors:

* **Server locations**: Look for a VPN with servers located close to Netflix's content libraries. This will help reduce latency and improve streaming quality.
* **Speed**: A fast VPN is essential for smooth streaming. Opt for a VPN that offers high-speed connections and low ping times.
* **Security**: Ensure the VPN uses strong encryption protocols, such as OpenVPN or WireGuard.
* **Compatibility**: Choose a VPN that supports your device's operating system (e.g., Windows, macOS, Android, iOS).
* **Customer support**: Look for a VPN with responsive customer support in case you encounter any issues.
