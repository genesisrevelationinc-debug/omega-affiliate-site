**How to Use Google Keyword Planner**

Google Keyword Planner is an incredibly powerful tool that's often overlooked by beginners. Here's how to use it:

1. **Create a campaign**: Head to the Google Ads dashboard and create a new campaign.
2. **Set up your project**: Choose "Tools" > "Keyword Planner" from the top menu.
3. **Enter keywords**: Type in keywords related to your niche or topic.
4. **Analyze results**: Look for metrics like search volume, competition, and cost-per-click (CPC).

**How to Use Google Trends**

Google Trends is a great way to identify popular topics and keywords. Here's how:

1. **Visit the Google Trends homepage**: Type in relevant keywords or phrases.
2. **Explore related topics**: Browse through the "Related topics" section for inspiration.
3. **Analyze search volume**: Look at the search volume over time to see if it's increasing or decreasing.
