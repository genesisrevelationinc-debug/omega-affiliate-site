# NordVPN vs ExpressVPN Comparison: Which VPN Reigns Supreme?

When it comes to choosing a Virtual Private Network (VPN), two names often come up in conversation: NordVPN and ExpressVPN. In this comprehensive comparison, we'll delve into the key differences between NordVPN and ExpressVPN to help you make an informed decision.

## Security Features

Both NordVPN and ExpressVPN prioritize user safety with robust security features. Here's a breakdown of their respective offerings:

*   **Encryption**: Both VPNs use AES-256 encryption, which is considered unbreakable by modern standards.
*   **Protocols**: NordVPN supports multiple protocols, including OpenVPN, IKEv2/IPsec, and WireGuard, while ExpressVPN uses its proprietary Lightway protocol in conjunction with OpenVPN.
*   **Kill Switch**: Both VPNs feature a kill switch that automatically disconnects your internet connection if the VPN drops.

However, NordVPN takes it to the next level with:

*   **Double VPN**: A unique feature that routes your traffic through two servers instead of one, adding an extra layer of protection against IP tracking.
*   ** Onion Over VPN**: This allows users to access the Tor network while maintaining the encryption and security benefits of a VPN.
