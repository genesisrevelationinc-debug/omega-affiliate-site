## What is CRM Software?

Before we dive into the top free CRM software options, let's quickly cover what CRM software is and why it's essential for businesses.

CRM software helps you manage customer interactions, track sales, and analyze data to make informed decisions. It typically includes features such as contact management, lead tracking, sales forecasting, and marketing automation. By using a CRM system, you can streamline your sales process, improve customer satisfaction, and increase revenue.
