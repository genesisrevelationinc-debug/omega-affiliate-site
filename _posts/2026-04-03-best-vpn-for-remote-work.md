**Why Do Remote Workers Need a VPN?**

Before diving into our top picks, let's quickly cover why a VPN is essential for remote workers:

*   **Data Encryption**: A VPN encrypts your internet traffic, ensuring that sensitive data remains confidential. This is particularly important when accessing public Wi-Fi networks or using unsecured connections.
*   **IP Address Masking**: By masking your IP address, you can maintain anonymity and avoid targeted ads, location-based tracking, or even identity theft.
*   **Censorship Circumvention**: A VPN helps bypass geo-restrictions and censorship, allowing you to access blocked websites, streaming services, or social media platforms.
