## What is a CRM?

Before we dive into the best CRMs for small businesses, let's quickly define what a CRM is:

A Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system is a software solution designed to manage all aspects of customer interactions. It helps you track leads, sales, and customer relationships, providing valuable insights to inform your business decisions.

## Choosing the Right CRM for Your Small Business

Now that we've explored some of the top CRMs, how do you choose the best one for your needs?

Here are a few key considerations:

2. **Customization:** Can the platform be tailored to meet your specific needs?
3. **Integration:** Does the platform integrate seamlessly with other applications and tools?
