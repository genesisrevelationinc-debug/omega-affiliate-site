**What is a CRM System?**

Before diving into the best free CRM software, let's quickly define what a CRM system is. A CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system helps businesses manage their interactions with customers, prospects, and partners. It stores data on customer interactions, including phone calls, emails, meetings, and sales activities.
