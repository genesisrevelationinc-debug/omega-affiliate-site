## What is Web Hosting?

Before we dive into the world of web hosting, let's quickly cover what it means. Web hosting is a service that allows individuals and organizations to host their websites on the internet. It provides storage space and bandwidth for your website to be accessible online. Think of it like renting a house - you pay a monthly fee, and the provider takes care of maintaining the property (in this case, your website).

## Types of Web Hosting

There are several types of web hosting to consider:

*   **Shared Hosting**: This is the most common type of hosting, where multiple websites share the same server. It's affordable but may not offer the best performance.
*   **VPS (Virtual Private Server) Hosting**: A VPS offers a dedicated portion of a server, providing more control and flexibility.
*   **Dedicated Hosting**: This is the most expensive option, where you rent an entire server for your website.

## What to Look for in Web Hosting

When choosing a web hosting provider, consider the following factors:

1.  **Uptime**: Ensure the provider has a high uptime guarantee (99% or higher).
2.  **Speed**: A fast load time is crucial for user experience and search engine rankings.
3.  **Security**: Look for providers with built-in security features, such as SSL certificates and firewalls.
4.  **Support**: Good customer support is essential, especially for beginners.
5.  **Pricing**: Balance affordability with the quality of service.
