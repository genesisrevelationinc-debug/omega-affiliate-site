## Top Free Online Learning Platforms

1.  **Coursera** ([www.coursera.org](https://coursera.org))
    Coursera offers a vast array of courses from top universities worldwide, covering subjects like computer science, business, data science, and more. With its flexible scheduling and affordable pricing options (some courses are free), it's an ideal platform for those looking to upskill or reskill.

2.  **edX** ([www.edx.org](https://edx.org))
    edX is another leading online learning platform that provides massive open online courses (MOOCs) from renowned institutions such as Harvard, MIT, and UC Berkeley. Its courses are often free, with the option to pay for verified certificates.

3.  **Udemy** ([www.udemy.com](https://udemy.com))
    While not all Udemy courses are free, it offers a wide selection of affordable courses on various subjects, including business, technology, and creative skills. Its flexible pricing model makes it an attractive option for those with specific learning needs.

4.  **OpenLearn** ([www.openlearn.com](https://openlearn.com))
    OpenLearn is The Open University's free online learning platform, offering a diverse range of courses on subjects like science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), arts, and humanities.

## Tools for Effective Learning

1.  **Quizlet**: A popular study app that allows users to create digital flashcards, play learning games, and take practice quizzes.
2.  **Khan Academy**: A free online platform offering video lectures on subjects like math, science, computer programming, and more.
3.  **Habits**: An app designed to help users track their daily habits, providing insights into how small changes can lead to significant improvements over time.
