## What is a VPN?

Before we dive into the specifics of NordVPN vs ExpressVPN, let's quickly cover what a VPN is. A VPN creates a secure, encrypted connection between your device and a VPN server, masking your IP address and protecting your online activities from prying eyes. This is particularly important for streaming services like Netflix, Hulu, and Amazon Prime, as well as when accessing public Wi-Fi networks.
