**Understanding Bitcoin**

Before we dive into the process of buying Bitcoin, it's essential to understand what it is. Bitcoin (BTC) is a decentralized digital currency that allows peer-to-peer transactions without the need for intermediaries like banks. It was created in 2009 by an individual or group using the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto.
