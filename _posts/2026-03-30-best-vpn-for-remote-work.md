## What is a VPN?

A VPN creates a secure, encrypted "tunnel" between your device and a VPN server, masking your IP address and protecting your online data from prying eyes. This is especially important when working remotely, as it ensures that your internet traffic remains private and secure, even on unsecured networks.

## Key Features to Look for in a VPN

When selecting the best VPN for remote work, there are several key features to consider:

*   **Server locations**: A wide range of servers in various countries can help you bypass geo-restrictions and access content from anywhere.
*   **Speed**: A fast VPN ensures that your online activities remain seamless and uninterrupted.
*   **Security**: Look for a VPN with robust encryption, such as AES-256, to protect your data.
*   **Ease of use**: A user-friendly interface makes it easy to set up and manage your VPN connection.
