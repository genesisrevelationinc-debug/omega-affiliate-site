## What is CRM Software?

Before we dive into the list of free CRM software, let's quickly define what CRM is and why it's essential for businesses. CRM software is designed to manage customer interactions, sales, and marketing activities across various channels. It helps businesses to:

* Track customer interactions and history
* Automate sales and marketing processes
* Analyze customer behavior and preferences
* Improve customer satisfaction and loyalty
