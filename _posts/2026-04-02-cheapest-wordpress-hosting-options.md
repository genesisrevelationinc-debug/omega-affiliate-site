## What is Shared Hosting?

Before diving into the cheapest options, let's quickly cover what shared hosting is. Shared hosting allows multiple websites to share resources on a single server. This means you'll be sharing disk space, bandwidth, and processing power with other websites. While it may not offer the same level of flexibility as VPS or dedicated hosting, shared hosting is an excellent option for small to medium-sized websites.
