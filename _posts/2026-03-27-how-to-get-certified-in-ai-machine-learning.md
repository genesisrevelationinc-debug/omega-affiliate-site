## Other Certification Programs

In addition to online courses, there are several other certification programs available:

### Certified Data Scientist (CDS) - Data Science Council Of America (DASCA)

The CDS certification is designed for professionals who want to demonstrate their expertise in data science. This certification covers a wide range of topics, including machine learning.

*   **Duration**: Self-paced
*   **Cost**: $299
*   **Certificate**: Yes

### Certified Machine Learning Engineer (CMLE) - International Association For Machine Learning And Artificial Intelligence (IAMAI)

The CMLE certification is designed for professionals who want to demonstrate their expertise in machine learning engineering. This certification covers a wide range of topics, including deep learning.

*   **Duration**: Self-paced
*   **Cost**: $299
*   **Certificate**: Yes
