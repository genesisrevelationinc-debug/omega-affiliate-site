# Best CRM For Small Business: Top Options Compared

As a small business owner, managing customer relationships can be overwhelming. With so many tools available, it's essential to find the right Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software that meets your needs. In this article, we'll explore the top CRMs for small businesses and provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to choose the best one.

## What is a CRM?

A CRM is a system used to manage interactions with current and potential customers. It helps businesses to organize contact information, track communications, and analyze customer behavior. A good CRM can streamline your sales process, improve customer satisfaction, and ultimately drive revenue growth.

### Key Features of a CRM

When selecting a CRM for your small business, consider the following essential features:

* Contact management
* Sales pipeline tracking
* Marketing automation
* Customer support ticketing system
* Analytics and reporting

### 3. Pipedrive CRM

Pipedrive is a sales-focused CRM that helps businesses to manage their sales pipeline and track deals. It's an excellent option for companies with complex sales processes.

* Pros:

	+ Customizable workflows

* Cons:
	+ Limited marketing automation features
	+ Pricing can be steep for large teams
