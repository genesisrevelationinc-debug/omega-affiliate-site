**What is CRM Software?**

Before diving into the best free CRM software options, let's quickly define what CRM software is. A CRM system is a tool that helps businesses manage customer interactions and data across various channels, such as email, phone, and social media. It's designed to improve sales, marketing, and customer service processes by providing insights and automating routine tasks.
