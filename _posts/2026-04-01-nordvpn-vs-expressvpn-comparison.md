## What is a VPN?

Before diving headfirst into the comparison, it's essential to understand what a VPN does. A VPN creates a secure, encrypted tunnel between your device and a VPN server, masking your IP address and location. This allows users to browse the internet privately, securely, and access geo-restricted content.
