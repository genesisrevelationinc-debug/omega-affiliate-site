### Security Tips for Buying Bitcoin

To ensure a safe buying experience:

* Use strong passwords and two-factor authentication.
* Keep your account information up-to-date.
* Monitor your transactions and report any suspicious activity.
* Consider using a hardware wallet for long-term storage.

## Storing Your Bitcoin

Once you've purchased Bitcoin, it's essential to store it securely. You can use a digital wallet on the exchange or opt for a hardware wallet for added security.

### Digital Wallets vs. Hardware Wallets

Consider the pros and cons of each option:

| **Digital Wallet** | **Hardware Wallet** |
| --- | --- |
| Pros: Easy access, convenient storage | Pros: High-security features, offline storage |
| Cons: Less secure, vulnerable to hacking | Cons: Requires manual setup, more expensive |
