## What Is Coursera?

Before we dive into the nitty-gritty, let's briefly cover what Coursera is. It's an online learning platform that partners with over 280 top universities and organizations worldwide, offering more than 7,000 courses across various subjects. From computer science to business, medicine, and humanities, you'll find a vast range of topics to suit your interests.

## Pricing: Is It Worth the Investment?

Coursera offers various pricing plans to suit different learning needs. Here's a breakdown:

*   **Audit:** Free, with limited access to course materials and no certification.
*   **Certificate:** $39-$79 per month, offering full access to course materials and certification upon completion.
*   **MasterTrack:** $1,200-$2,500, providing a more comprehensive learning experience with personalized guidance.

While Coursera's pricing may seem steep at first glance, consider the value you receive:

*   Expert instructors from top universities
*   Flexible learning schedule
*   Opportunities to connect with peers worldwide
