## What Makes a Good VPN?

A good VPN should possess several key features:

1.  **Strong Encryption**: The ability to protect data transmitted over public networks.
2.  **Server Network**: Access to a large network of servers worldwide for optimal performance.
3.  **Ease of Use**: A user-friendly interface that makes setup and usage straightforward.
4.  **Compatibility**: Support for various platforms, including desktops, laptops, smartphones, and tablets.

NordVPN meets these criteria, boasting robust encryption, an extensive server network, a simple installation process, and compatibility with multiple devices.
