## What is Web Hosting?

Before we dive into the best options, let's start with the basics. Web hosting is a service that allows individuals or organizations to upload their website files to a server on the internet. This makes your website accessible to anyone with an internet connection. Think of it like renting space in a virtual storage facility for your website.

## Key Factors to Consider

When choosing a web host, there are several key factors to consider:

*   **Uptime**: Look for hosts with high uptime guarantees (95% or higher) to ensure your site is always accessible.
*   **Speed**: Choose hosts that offer fast loading times and optimized server performance.
*   **Customer Support**: Select hosts with 24/7 support, including phone, email, and live chat options.
*   **Scalability**: Opt for hosts that can grow with your website's needs.
*   **Security**: Consider hosts that offer free SSL certificates, malware scanning, and regular backups.
