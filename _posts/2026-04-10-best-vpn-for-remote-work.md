**What Makes a Good VPN for Remote Work?**

Before diving into specific VPN recommendations, let's discuss what makes an excellent VPN for remote work:

1.  **Strong encryption**: Look for AES-256 or higher encryption to ensure your data is secure.
2.  **Multiple servers**: A large server network ensures you can connect from anywhere in the world without speed issues.
3.  **High-speed connections**: Choose a VPN with fast speeds, especially if you work with demanding tasks like video editing or online gaming.
4.  **User-friendly interface**: A simple and intuitive interface makes it easy to set up and use your VPN.

**Additional Features to Consider**

When choosing a VPN for remote work, consider the following additional features:

*   **Split tunneling**: Allows you to choose which apps use the VPN and which don't.
*   **Kill switch**: Automatically disconnects your internet if the VPN connection drops.
*   **Multi-platform support**: Ensure the VPN is available on all devices you use.
