## What is a VPN?

Before diving into the best VPNs, let's briefly explain what they are and how they work. A VPN creates a secure, encrypted connection between your device and a VPN server. This connection masks your IP address, making it difficult for hackers to track your online activities.

VPNs are essential for:

* **Secure browsing**: Protect yourself from malware, phishing scams, and other cyber threats.
* **Private surfing**: Hide your location and prevent data collection by advertisers.
* **Secure streaming**: Access geo-restricted content without worrying about buffering or lag.
