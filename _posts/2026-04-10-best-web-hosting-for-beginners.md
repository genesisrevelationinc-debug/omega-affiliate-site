## What is Web Hosting?

Before we dive into the world of web hosting, let's take a quick detour to understand what it's all about. Simply put, web hosting refers to the service that allows individuals or organizations to host their websites on the internet. This involves storing your website's files and data on servers connected to the internet, making it accessible to anyone with an internet connection.

## Key Features to Consider

When choosing a web host, there are several key features to consider:

1.  **Uptime**: Look for providers that offer high uptime guarantees (e.g., 99.9% or higher).
2.  **Speed**: Opt for hosts with fast page loading speeds to improve user experience.
3.  **Storage and Bandwidth**: Ensure the host offers sufficient storage space and bandwidth for your website's needs.
4.  **Security**: Look for providers that offer robust security measures, such as free SSL certificates and regular backups.
5.  **Customer Support**: Choose hosts with reliable customer support, including live chat, phone, and email assistance.
