**What is a VPN?**

Before diving into the best VPNs for streaming Netflix, let's briefly explain what a VPN is and how it works. A VPN creates a secure, encrypted tunnel between your device and the internet, masking your IP address and location. This makes it difficult for ISPs to throttle or block access to certain websites, including streaming services like Netflix.

**Why Do You Need a VPN for Streaming Netflix?**

While Netflix is available in over 190 countries worldwide, its content library varies greatly depending on the region. If you're traveling abroad or want to access exclusive content from another country, a VPN can help you bypass geo-restrictions and access your favorite shows and movies.

Additionally, ISPs often throttle streaming services like Netflix to manage bandwidth and prevent congestion. A VPN can help you avoid ISP throttling and maintain a stable connection for smooth streaming.
